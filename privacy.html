KQuiz Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy explains how KQuiz handles information when you use the app, including local study data, selected files, camera access for QR scanning, advertising, and optional AI Pro processing.

Effective date: April 24, 2026 Offline-first app No account required

1. Information stored in the app

KQuiz is designed to work primarily on your device. Study sets, flashcards, progress, review history, theme selection, achievements, and other learning data are stored locally on your device unless a specific feature clearly requires an online request.

2. Files and documents you choose to import

When you select a file such as PDF, TXT, DOCX, PPTX, XLSX, JSON, or STUDYSET, KQuiz reads only the content needed to process that file inside the app. These files are not uploaded to our server by default.

3. Camera access

KQuiz requests camera permission only for features that need it, such as scanning QR codes to import shared study sets. If you do not grant camera permission, you can still use the file import flow instead.

4. Advertising

KQuiz uses Google AdMob to show ads, including rewarded ads for selected premium actions. AdMob may collect device and advertising information in accordance with Google policies. Please review Google's privacy information if you want more details about how AdMob processes data.

5. Optional AI Pro processing

KQuiz includes an optional AI Pro mode for the “Import from file” flow. AI Pro is only used when you explicitly choose that mode and unlock it through the in-app rewarded flow. In that case, the extracted text or cleaned document content required for quiz generation may be sent to a cloud AI backend for processing. If AI Pro is unavailable, fails, or you do not choose it, the app falls back to local processing.

6. Third-party services

KQuiz may rely on third-party services for specific features, including:

  • Google AdMob for advertising
  • Optional cloud AI provider and proxy backend for AI Pro
  • Platform-level Android components needed for file access, camera, and sharing

7. Children's privacy

KQuiz is a general education app and is not intentionally targeted at children under the age where parental consent is legally required in your region. If you believe information was provided inappropriately, please contact us.

8. Security

We try to keep KQuiz practical and privacy-conscious by storing learning data locally where possible and limiting cloud processing to clearly optional features. However, no storage or transmission method can be guaranteed to be 100% secure.

9. Changes to this Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy when KQuiz changes its features, integrations, or data handling. The latest version will be published on this page.
